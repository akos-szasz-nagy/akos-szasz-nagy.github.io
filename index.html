How I work?

00 — Discovery

Learn about customer needs, collect pain-points. Define a user story with the Product Manager, discuss implementation challenges with Engineers. Consult the WCAG guidelines.

01 — Audit

Explore ideas, investigate industry standards and best practices on the market. Check WAI patterns.

02 — Ideation and Feasibility

Rough concepts, continuous involvement of Engineers about Feasibility issues, agree on specs.

03 — Validation

Peer review, unmoderated testing of prototypes, iterate if needed. Revisit the original pain points.

04 — Handoff

Document design specs and notate accessibility considerations (e.g. tab order, screen reader announcement, keyboard behaviour, etc.)

05 — Measure

After release, measure the usability of the feature continuously.
